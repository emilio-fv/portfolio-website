About

Hello! My name is Emilio and I’m a cybersecurity analyst who is driven by passion for developing impactful and sustainable security solutions for a safer digital and physical world. A background as a classically trained violinist, music teacher, and arts administrator taught me how to approach challenges from different perspectives, the value of resourcefulness, and how to collaborate with people from different backgrounds. My journey into tech began in 2022 with a simple online coding class that sparked the realization that I can use many of the creative skills I’ve developed as a musician to solve real world problems. My primary goal now is to work as a cybersecurity analyst for a company or organization that is driven by serving communities, open to new ideas and challenges, and working to build a more secure world.

I'm always happy to connect with other creative and technical minded people, so feel free to reach out!
